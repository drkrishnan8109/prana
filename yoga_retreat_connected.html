Find your stillness between leaf and wave

Our Philosophy

Rooted in nature,
open to the sky

Every practice at Prana Grove is guided by three pillars of holistic living — body, breath, and belonging.

🌿

Forest Immersion

Open-air pavilions surrounded by ancient trees. Let the forest breathe with you. Morning fog, birdsong, and bark underfoot.

🌊

Ocean Breathwork

Tide-synced pranayama sessions on the shoreline at sunrise and sunset. The ocean teaches us about expansion and return.

🧘

Expert Guidance

Certified teachers and visiting guest hosts with decades of lineage-based training across Hatha, Vinyasa, Yin, and Kundalini.

🍃

Conscious Nourishment

Plant-forward meals sourced from our on-site garden and local farmers. Eating as practice, nourishment as ceremony.
